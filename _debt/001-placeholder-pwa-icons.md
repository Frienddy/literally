# DEBT-001 — Placeholder PWA icons

**Status:** Open (deferred) · **Severity:** Low · **Surfaced by:** PRD-001 (Platform Shell & PWA)

> **Reviewed 2026-06-21 (tech-debt pass): still deferred.** Blocked on the
> mascot/brand direction, which is explicitly **P2 / v1.1** (PRD-000 §6, OQ-11) and
> unowned — there is no brand art to ship, so any work now would be thrown away.
> Resolve when the mascot art lands; no code action available in the meantime.

## What

The PWA icons (`public/icons/icon-192.png`, `icon-512.png`, `maskable-512.png`)
are programmatic placeholders — a solid accent-blue dot on the app's `#0b1020`
ink background, generated by `scripts/generate-icons.mjs` (zero-dependency raw
PNG encoder, run via `npm run icons`).

They are valid, correctly sized, and the maskable variant keeps its content inside
the safe zone, so installability and Lighthouse pass. They are **not** real
branding.

## Why deferred

PRD-001 only requires *installable, correctly-specified* icons. Real artwork
depends on the mascot/brand direction, which is explicitly **P2 / v1.1** in
PRD-000 §6 ("richer art for the mascot") and unowned by a Phase-0 PRD. Producing
brand art now would be out of scope and likely thrown away.

## Risk

Cosmetic only. The home-screen icon and splash look unbranded. No functional or
accessibility impact.

## Suggested resolution

When brand/mascot art lands (P2, see OQ-11 in `_docs/10`), replace the three PNGs
(and the `apple-touch-icon` reference in `index.html`). Either drop final assets
into `public/icons/` or extend `scripts/generate-icons.mjs` to render the real
mark. Keep the 192 / 512 / maskable-512 set and the maskable safe zone.
